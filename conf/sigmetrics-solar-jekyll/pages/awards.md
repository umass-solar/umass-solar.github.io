## The SIGMETRICS Achievement Award (snapshot)
The ACM SIGMETRICS Achievement Award is given annually to an individual who has made long-lasting, influential contributions to the analysis and evaluation of computer/communication system performance.

**Typical nomination packet (summary):**
- A 2-page description of qualifying accomplishments and their significance
- CV of the nominee
- Five endorsements (with community membership/publication constraints)
- A concise plaque inscription statement

For full details, deadlines, and committee information, refer to the original page:
- https://sigmetrics.org/awards.shtml
